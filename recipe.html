Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packened brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extraxt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375degF
  2. In a small bowl, mix flour, baking soda and salt.Set aside
  3. In a large bowl,beat butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar untilcreamy.
  4. Beats in eggs and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually blend in flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons onto ungreesed baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes;remove to wire rack to cool completely.
